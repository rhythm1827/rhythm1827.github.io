Biography

Assalam Alaikum, I am S. M. Ruhul Kabir Howlader. I am a PhD in Computer Science (Driverleics AI: Federated Edge Artificial Intelligence (AI) for Autonomous Vehicles) student at the University of Leicester. I have served almost 3.5 years as an Artificial Intelligence Engineer and Software Engineer at two renowned IT companies in Bangladesh. I completed my M. Sc. in Computer Science from American International University - Bangladesh (AIUB) and B. Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering from East West University (EWU), Dhaka, Bangladesh. I received Summa Cum Laude from AIUB in the 20th convocation. I was an Undergraduate Teaching Assistant(UTA) at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at East West University. During my B. Sc. I achieved the Dean Scholarship and Medha Lalon Scholarship from East West University. I worked on several projects on Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Computer Vision, Image Processing, HCI, NLP, and Web Development.
